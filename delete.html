How to Delete Your LWP Account

Follow these steps to delete your account from our Android app:

  1. Step 1: Open the app and log in to your account.
  2. Step 2: After logging in, navigate to the home page.
  3. Step 3: In the bottom navigation view, click on the center profile button.
  4. Step 4: On the profile screen, you will see the logout button and the "Delete My Account" form.
  5. Step 5: Confirm your action by entering your password.
  6. Step 6: Tap on "Delete" to finalize the process.

Note: Once you delete your account, all your data will be permanently removed and cannot be recovered.
